Entrance Exam Preparation Strategy

JEE Main/Advanced

Subjects: Physics • Chemistry • Mathematics

High

Preparation Tips:

  • Focus on NCERT concepts
  • Practice mock tests daily
  • Time management is crucial

NEET

Subjects: Physics • Chemistry • Biology

High

Preparation Tips:

  • Master NCERT thoroughly
  • Focus on Biology (50% weightage)
  • Practice MCQs extensively

CUET

Subjects: Domain subjects • General test • Language

Moderate

Preparation Tips:

  • Align with your stream
  • Stay updated with current affairs
  • Practice language skills
